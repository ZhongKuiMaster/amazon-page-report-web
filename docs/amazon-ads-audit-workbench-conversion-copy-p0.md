# Amazon Ads Audit Workbench Conversion Copy P0

## Page Positioning

This page sells a semi-automated, expert-reviewed Amazon Ads diagnosis. It should not read like a free calculator, a fully automated SaaS, or an agency retainer page.

Get a paid ads diagnosis

Secondary CTA:

See what the audit includes

Support line near CTA:

## Who It Is For

- Amazon sellers spending on Sponsored Products but unsure which spend is helping.
- Operators who need a clear next-week action queue, not a generic PPC checklist.
- Brand owners trying to reduce wasted spend without cutting profitable growth.
- Sellers preparing for a deal, rank push, or launch window and needing ad decisions backed by evidence.
- Teams that want a second opinion before changing bids, budgets, targets, or campaign structure.

## What You Need To Prepare

- One Sponsored Products search term or campaign performance report.
- Main ASIN or SKU group you want diagnosed.
- Target ACOS or TACOS, or your break-even ACOS if you know it.
- Basic business context: marketplace, goal, margin, weekly sales, conversion rate, inventory, and promo window if available.
- Any notes on current pain: wasted spend, weak ROAS, rank push, brand defense, deal support, or sales recovery.

## What You Will Receive

- A clear read on where ad spend is leaking or underperforming.
- Search term, campaign, and ad group actions ranked by priority.
- Budget and bid direction for the next operating window.
- Warnings when the issue may be listing conversion, margin, inventory, Featured Offer, or promo timing rather than ads alone.
- A next 7-day action queue with evidence, confidence, and recommended owner.
- A short explanation of missing data and what would improve diagnosis confidence.

## What This Is Not

- This is not automatic campaign management.
- This is not an Amazon Ads account connection or OAuth tool.
- This is not a guarantee that ACOS will drop or sales will increase.
- This is not a full account audit across every ASIN, marketplace, and report type.
- This is not a replacement for ongoing operator judgment after the audit.

## Short FAQ

### Do I need to connect my Amazon Ads account?

No. The P0 version uses your exported Sponsored Products report and a short business-context form.

### What report should I upload first?

Start with a Sponsored Products search term report or campaign performance report for the ASIN or SKU group you want diagnosed.

### How fast will I receive the audit?

Standard delivery is within 24 hours after we receive a valid report and the required business context.

### Will you change my campaigns for me?

No. You receive a reviewed diagnosis and action queue. You decide whether to apply the changes.

### Can you diagnose without margin or TACOS target?

Yes, but confidence is lower. We can still identify spend waste and query quality, but profit-based decisions are better when margin or break-even ACOS is available.

## Do Not Put This Sentence On The Page

"Connect your Amazon Ads account and we will automatically lower your ACOS."

Why not: it overpromises automation, implies campaign mutation that is not implemented, and suggests a guaranteed outcome the product should not claim.
